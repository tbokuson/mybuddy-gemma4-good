## Recording Checklist

- Avoid showing personal photos or private diary data.
- Keep all captions readable on mobile.
- Do not claim medical, therapy, diagnostic, or loneliness treatment effects.
- Upload the video as public or unlisted so Kaggle judges can view it without login.
